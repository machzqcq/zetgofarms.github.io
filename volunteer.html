--- title: Volunteer hero: Volunteer With Us ---

Independent farmers like us ALWAYS appreciate volunteering services and any help

Volunteer

Below are how you can engage with us and some tasks

  • Moving things and resources - There are activities like this throughout the year
  • Germination activities - We continuously seed-to-sapling-transfer-harvest cycle
  • Harvesting activities - Harvest vegetables, wash them and organize into storage etc.
  • Marketing & Selling - Can involve both digital and physical

Note

A liability waiver has to be signed by all adults who step on the farm and help us

Please reach out to us on your availability via the "Contact" menu item (header or footer menu)
